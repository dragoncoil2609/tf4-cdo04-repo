## Final acceptance rule

Do not claim final acceptance success until regenerated evidence proves:

```text
prediction_source = AI_ENGINE
evidence_status = complete_window
ai_status_code = 200
```

Additional hard gates:

- 4 scenario decisions recorded.
- >=3 services covered.
- Recall/catch >= 80%.
- FP rate <= 12%.
- F1 and confusion matrix reported.
- Brier score or reliability bins reported.
- At least one >=2h scenario shows lead time >=15 minutes.
- 50/100 RPS acceptance load passes.
- Security/isolation probes pass or are explicitly N/A for single sandbox.
- DLQ no growth and ECS services stable.
- Budget/cost evidence stays under $200 cap.
